Adds covariate effect to pharmpy model.

effect - Supports linear (continuous and categorical covariates),

piecewise linear, exponential, and power function. Custom effect may be used, where thetas are denoted as ‘theta’ (if multiple: ‘theta1’, ‘theta2’ etc), covariate as ‘cov’, and mean and median are written as they are.
