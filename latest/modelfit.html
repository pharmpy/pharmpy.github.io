Modelfit¶

Pharmpy can retrieve results from a NONMEM run.

\[\]

Modelfit results¶

Final OFV¶

The final OFV is available in ofv:

from pharmpy.modeling import read_model

model = read_model('tests/testdata/nonmem/pheno_real.mod')
model.modelfit_results.ofv

586.2760562818805
