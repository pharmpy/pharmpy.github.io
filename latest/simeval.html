Individual OFV summary#

The iofv_summary table contain the iOFVs from the orginal model, the mean and standard deviation of the sampled iOFVs, the residual and a residual outlier flag. The residual for each sample and ID is the distance from the observed iOFV to the simulated iOFV expressed in standard deviations of the simulated values.

\[\mathrm{res} = \frac{\mathrm{obs} - \operatorname{mean}(\mathrm{sim})}{\operatorname{sd}(\mathrm{sim})}\]

An individual is defined as an outlier if the corresponding residual is 3 or higher.
